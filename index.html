Twenty years from now, a new technology emerges, allowing everyday people to rent out a portion of their brain’s processing power. As more people become convinced of the technology’s adverse psychological effects, the group of friends behind the MindShare Oppositional Action Network plan direct actions against the company. But when the group becomes close with Lucy, an artificial intelligence trying to find her way in the world, they must reevaluate their priorities and acknowledge the ways in which they, too, have been complacent with MindShare’s actions.

All in Your Mind is a musical with book by Joey Birdsong and music by Jake Berran and Jacob Richter. The musical was first produced by the Oberlin Student Theater Association in April 2024 at Oberlin College.
